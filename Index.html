Facts about Pwani University

Pwani University is a public university located in the coastal region of kenya. Approximately, it is 60km far North of Mombasa in Kilifi County. The University occupies approximately 239 hectares and was founded on 23 August 2007. Within the few years of operation, the instituion has been able to accomodate and facilitate 7 schools of study. In most cases, the classes start at 7.00 am and commences at 8.00pm. Part of Kilifi is the Indian Ocean and this gives the students the priviledge to enjoy vast of beaches within the region.
